The project aimed to make legal information accessible, accurate, and user-friendly, especially for individuals and businesses navigating complex legal landscapes.
The interface offers users a seamless experience with dynamic dark and light modes, ensuring readability and ease of use across various devices.
The app includes real-time message streaming capabilities, allowing for a smooth, conversational flow of information.
